# How to Prepare for an Interview

> **Coming soon.** This guide is planned. In the meantime, run `/career-ops interview-prep` with a company name inside Claude Code.

This guide covers using Career-Ops to build interview preparation material for a specific company and role, including STAR+R stories and a running story bank.

**What this covers:**

- Running `/career-ops interview-prep` for a target company
- Understanding the STAR+R story format (Situation, Task, Action, Result, Reflection)
- How the story bank in `interview-prep/story-bank.md` accumulates across evaluations
- Reading and using company-specific intel reports in `interview-prep/`
- Updating the story bank after an interview with what actually worked
