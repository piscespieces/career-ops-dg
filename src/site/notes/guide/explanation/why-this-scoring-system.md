# Why This Scoring System

> **Coming soon.** This explanation is planned.

This page explains the philosophy behind Career-Ops' evaluation approach — why it scores the way it does, what values it encodes, and why quality over quantity is a core constraint rather than a preference.

**What this covers:**

- Why the system recommends against applying to anything below 4.0/5
- The ethical argument: your time and the recruiter's time are both finite
- Why keyword matching was rejected in favor of reasoning about fit
- How the 6-block structure was designed to cover all the dimensions that actually matter in a job search (not just technical match)
- Why the system never auto-submits: the human-in-the-loop principle
- The tradeoff between breadth (scanning many jobs) and depth (evaluating each one seriously)
- Why the system gets smarter over time and what "smarter" actually means here
